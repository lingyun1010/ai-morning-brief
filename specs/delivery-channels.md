# Delivery Channels

The Lingyun AI Morning Brief has three target delivery channels.

## Gmail

Gmail is the primary delivery channel.

Requirements:

- Complete HTML email
- `html_body` or `content_type='text/html'`
- Mobile-friendly layout
- Hero and Market Note modules
- All 10 sections
- Source links
- Separate LinkedIn and X content cards

## Notion

Notion is the archive and working knowledge base.

Requirements:

- One page per daily brief
- Date and title
- All 10 sections
- Source links
- Today's 1-Hour Action
- Useful tags for search and review

Suggested tags:

- `AI Morning Brief`
- `AI Engineering`
- `Builder Gallery`
- `Open Source`
- `Job Market`

## Slack

Slack is the quick-scan version.

Requirements:

- Daily headline
- 4-6 bullet executive summary
- Top 3 source links
- Today's 1-Hour Action
- Notion link if available

Slack should be concise. It does not need the full newsletter.
