# Source Strategy

The brief should feel current, credible, and builder-aware. Use a mix of primary and secondary sources.

## Priority Sources

- Official AI company blogs and release notes
- GitHub repositories, release notes, issues, and examples
- Product Hunt launches
- Hacker News discussions
- X/Twitter release threads from builders
- YouTube demos
- Personal technical blogs
- Reputable AI and technology publications
- Company career pages and job posts
- Funding and company announcements

## Diversity Requirements

Each daily brief must include:

- At least 2 authoritative AI/news sources
- At least 2 builder/product sources
- At least 2 GitHub/open-source sources
- At least 1 AI job-market/company source

## Recency Rules

Prefer sources from the last 7 days. Older sources are allowed only when they explain or verify something being discussed today.

## Link Rules

Prefer original links over commentary:

- Use the GitHub repo, not only an article about the repo.
- Use the launch post, not only a repost.
- Use the official announcement, not only social commentary.
- Use the original demo video when available.

Secondary discussion links can be included when they add useful context.
