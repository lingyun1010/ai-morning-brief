# Sample Gmail Preview Notes

Use these notes when reviewing a rendered Gmail draft.

## First Impression

- The hero should be visible immediately on mobile.
- The headline should feel specific to today.
- The email should read like an editorial briefing, not a pasted Markdown document.

## Mobile Scan

- Cards should stack in a single column.
- Text should be readable without zooming.
- Links should be easy to tap.
- Section headings should be visually distinct.

## Content Checks

- Hero, Market Note, and Sections 1-10 are present.
- Section 4 has exactly 5 real published projects.
- LinkedIn and X ideas are separate.
- Source links are visible.
- Today's 1-Hour Action is concrete.

## Common Failures

- Markdown syntax appears in the email body.
- Source links are missing or vague.
- Section 4 has fewer or more than 5 projects.
- The email looks like a raw ChatGPT answer instead of a newsletter.
- Visual placeholders are missing.
